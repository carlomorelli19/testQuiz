## Core Narrative

### The Problem
The experimentation phase of AI is over. Most B2B companies have:
- Long idea lists
- Ongoing pilots
- Surface-level tools live in production

But the **real breakthrough is missing**. The gap between experimentation and genuine, measurable commercial impact has never been wider — or more urgent to close.

### The Goal
Move past fragmented pilots. Build a cohesive, high-impact, AI-enabled commercial engine that solves actual business friction.

### The Promise
> *"No fluffy theories. 100% actionable insights."*

## Content Pillars

### Pillar A — Benchmarking AI Maturity
Help attendees gauge where their organisation stands vs. the market.

**Maturity model:**
```
Ad-hoc Tooling → Pilot / Siloed Execution → Integrated Agentic Engines
```

---

### Pillar B — Current State of AI in B2B Commercial Operations
Dispel hype. Show what is working right now in 2026.

Key shift: **beyond ChatGPT / generic LLMs** → automated workflows and proactive operations.

---

### Pillar C — High-Impact AI Agent Use Cases

**Customer Interactions**
- Personalised recommendations
- Automated hyper-targeted follow-ups
- Agentic commerce experiences

**Internal Processes**
- Smarter automated lead scoring
- Real-time sales pipeline automation
- Automated deal logging / speech data entry

**Data Insights**
- Identifying underlying trends
- Predictive churn modelling
- Serving real-time actionable insights to sales reps

---

### Pillar D — Solutions to Tech and Data Infrastructure Challenges
Address the underlying blockers holding back AI success.

Key topics:
- Data structure and architecture (e.g. Data Cloud grounding)
- Pipeline scalability
- Why **70% of AI effort** goes into testing/refining rather than building

---

### Pillar E — Compliance and Regulation Demystified
Remove fear and ambiguity around security, compliance, and privacy.

Focus: clear, actionable guardrails for deploying AI safely in B2B enterprise landscapes.
